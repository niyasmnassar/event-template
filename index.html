What we offer you

When you get married in Kerala, you need to get your game face ON. Not only are you tackling super early morning schedules, strict timelines and stricter clergy, you are also managing long distance travel to get to venues while thinking about your wedding guests who’ve traveled for hours just to be a part of your wedding day.

Well, we at Ivory Events take the stress part out for you!!!!!!

You don’t have to be bothered by the nitty gritty details of the function like how many watts you need for the music system or what kind of power generator you require as a backup in case of a power failure or how the tables need to be set in the reception venue to accommodate your guests.

There are a whole lot of things that would have you pulling out your hair when you’re dealing with a multitude of vendors & things don’t fall into place at the time you expect them to.

Leave the booking, co-ordination & executing to us and we will guarantee you a unperturbed wedding for you to attend to your dear ones. We will be your on-site coordinator for the management and implementation of the variety of preparations on the day of the wedding as well as the day before.
